Stopped Watch

Charcoal Pencil, c. 2023.

A4 (8.27 in x 11.69 in)

This painting represents the cruelty and devastation of war, with white symbolizing the past and black symbolizing the present. Through the glass shards of the broken pocket watch, we can learn about the past of the owner of this hand, and it shows in detail how his life and daily life are shattered by war. At the same time, the extreme contrast between the present and the past through color divides the past and present even further, distorting and degenerating them into something unrelated. It is as if his pocket watch has broken and stopped, his past has stopped forever, and since time no longer flows, his future will also no longer flow.

Dream No More

Acrylic Painting, c. 2023

30 cm × 19.7 cm (11.8 in × 7.76 in)

This is a painting depicting the true ending which is 'Dream No More' in my favorite game, Hollow Knight. The final enemy, The Radiance, is depicted as the god of dreams and the sun itself, and an intense light emanates from her face. Therefore, I used the hole in the palette as her face, and when I put this painting up in the sky, we can see the sun's light pouring through the hole. This is a work that contains my genius idea and intention, with perfect historical scientific and directing, and although it does not have any specific message or philosophical elements, the directing is very wonderful.

Personally, this is a work that I really like.
